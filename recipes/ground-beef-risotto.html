Ground Beef Risotto

Weekday risotto is easy to prepare in one frying pan in about half an hour. Ground beef and rice are the main ingredients in this easy-to-make dish that the whole family will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 400 g of beef mince, 10% fat
  • 1 pepper, diced
  • 100 g of onion, chopped
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 1 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 3 dl of long-grain rice
  • ½ - 1 tsp of crushed black pepper
  • 4 ½ dl of water
  • 1 can (250 g) of Valio Koskenlaskija Food & Dip, unflavored
  • 1 tsp of basil
  • 100 g of cherry tomatoes

Steps

  1. Brown the beef mince in a frying pan. Add the diced pepper and chopped onion. Cook until the onion is translucent. Crumble the stock cube on top and season with pepper.
  2. Add the tomato paste and rice. Stir for a moment.
  3. Add water and heat. Let the rice cook covered while stirring occasionally for 10-15 minutes. Cooking time depends on the type of rice.
  4. Add Koskenlaskija and basil. Heat. Finish with diced tomatoes.
  5. Serve the ground beef risotto with salad and a drink.
